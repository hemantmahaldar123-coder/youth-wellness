What to Do in a Crisis

If someone is at risk of self-harm or suicide:

1. Call 112 immediately if medical help is needed

2. Stay with the person (if it's safe to do so)

3. Remove any immediate means of harm

4. Call a counselling helpline for guidance

5. Contact their family or nearest hospital
